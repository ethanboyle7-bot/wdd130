Courses

Half Day Adevnture

One of our beginner courses. While it may be a bit long, our expert guides will teach you all the skills you need to progress on your White Water journey. Perfect for beginners and families alike to enjoy together with less danger than the advanced courses.

Big Mallard

An intermediate course designed for those who are looking to experience more of a challenge and are not afraid of the thrill that White Water Rafting can bring. Big Mallard will test the skills you have learnt and put you and your team to the test in wetter and wilder rapids than you may be used to.

Los Dientes del Diablos

This course is not for the faint of heart. An advanced course only recommeneded for the most serious and dedicated rafters. Los Dientes del Diablos will put you to the test and you must use all your skills to conquer it. Our expert guides will give you all the assistance you need to defeat this course, if you dare!
